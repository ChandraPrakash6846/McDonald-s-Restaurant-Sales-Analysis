# McDonald's Restaurant Sales Analysis

## 3 Project Overview
An interactive Excel dashboard analyzing McDonald's sales data to uncover key business insights and drive data-driven decision-making. This project transforms raw sales data into actionable strategies for optimizing operations, marketing, and inventory management.

##  Business Objectives
- Analyze sales performance and revenue trends across menu categories
- Identify customer ordering patterns and peak business hours
- Understand weekday vs. weekend sales dynamics
- Provide an interactive tool for ongoing business intelligence

##  Key Insights Discovered

### Revenue Drivers
- **Burgers** dominate as the primary revenue generator ($21,639.01)
- **Chicken** and **Pasta** categories show strong performance
- Top 3 categories account for ~70% of total revenue

### Temporal Patterns
- **Weekdays generate 72%** of total sales ($44,475.75 vs $17,150.54 weekends)
- Peak ordering during **lunch (12-2 PM)** and **dinner (6-8 PM)** hours
- Monday shows highest order volume (~17% of weekly sales)

### Customer Behavior
- Average of **59.67 orders per day**
- **2.28 items per order** average
- Breakfast, Shakes, and Wraps identified as underperforming categories

##  Technologies Used
- **Microsoft Excel** with Advanced Formulas and Pivot Tables
- **Interactive Dashboard** with Slicers and Dynamic Charts
- **Data Cleaning Techniques**: Duplicate removal, null value handling

##  Dashboard Features
- Category-wise revenue breakdown
- Time-based sales analysis (hourly, daily, monthly)
- Weekday vs. Weekend performance comparison
- Top-performing items ranking
- Interactive filters for dynamic data exploration

##  Strategic Recommendations
1. **Maximize Weekday Dominance**: Optimize staffing and create express lunch combos
2. **Boost Weekend Performance**: Introduce family bundles and weekend specials
3. **Category Optimization**: Leverage burger popularity with premium offerings
4. **Menu Strategy**: Promote high-margin items during peak hours

##  Business Impact
This analysis enables:
- Data-driven staffing and scheduling decisions
- Optimized inventory management based on sales patterns
- Targeted marketing strategies for underperforming categories
- Enhanced customer satisfaction through peak-hour optimization
